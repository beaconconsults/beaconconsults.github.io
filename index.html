The Problem

Most inventory problems
are invisible until they hurt

You're running the business. You know something feels off — cash is tighter than the numbers suggest, you're running out of the wrong things, and buying decisions feel like guesswork. Here's why.

🔒

Trapped Cash

Slow-moving and dead stock is the silent cash drain most businesses don't measure. It's not a write-off — it's capital that could be deployed into faster-moving SKUs, payroll, or growth. The average SMB product business has 15–25% of its inventory value locked in stock that hasn't moved in 90+ days.

🚨

Stockout Risk

Running out of your best sellers doesn't just cost you the sale. It costs you the customer. Most stockouts are visible weeks in advance when you know where to look — but without a structured process, the first signal is an empty shelf and a missed order.

📊

Buying Blind

When inventory decisions are made on instinct and rough estimates, the result is overbuying on slow movers and underbuying on winners. Turns, DOI, and GMROI turn gut feel into a clear, SKU-level picture of where every dollar is performing — and where it isn't.
